# RTFOAM v10

Version 10 replaces the shared generic aircraft silhouette with four dedicated deterministic geometry families: Trainer, Fighter, Experimental flying wing, and Warbird. The selected style is carried into every hangar preview, dossier, and cut sheet, including older saved designs.

Key changes:
- style-specific top, exploded, perspective, and dossier SVG geometry
- style-specific true-scale cut sheets
- warbird elliptical low-wing geometry with fuller fuselage and tail
- fighter swept/delta geometry with pointed fuselage and appropriate fins/tail
- experimental flying-wing geometry with center equipment pod and twin fins
- trainer high-wing geometry with rounded tips and long conventional tail
- architecture-specific dossier labels and assembly sequence
- renderer and application version synchronization at v10
